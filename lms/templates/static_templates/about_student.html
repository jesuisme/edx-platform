Performance Tracking Dashboard




Welcome to the Student Performance Dashboard. This dashboard is meant to be used to track your progress on pre and post tests as well as your certificates and badges, and some data on how often you log in and spend time viewing content.
Each of the dashboard elements are defined below:

Module views per day bar chart: Bar chart with day on the x axis and number of module views on the y-axis. This chart tells you how many times you have logged in and viewed at least one page within a module.

Logins per day bar chart: Bar chart with day on the x axis and number of logins on the y-axis. This chart tells you how often you have logged into this website over time.

Module Progress: How far along you are in this course as defined by the number of sections you have completed divided by the total number of sections in the course

Pre-test: Your score on the pre test for this course out of 100%. Defined as the number of questions correct/total number of questions.

Final test: Your score on the final test for this course out of 100%. Defined as the number of questions correct/total number of questions.

Badge and Certificates Earned: The total number of badges and certificates you have earned for this course. There is only one certificate available per course and multiple badges are possible per course.

Average time spent per log in: The mean time you spent logged into the website across all of your logins in minutes.
