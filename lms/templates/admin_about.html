Cohorts Performance Dashboard


Welcome to the Administrator Cohort Management Dashboard. Below are definitions of the elements included on this dashboard.

Cohorts: The number of cohorts you have created.

Modules Assigned: The total number of modules assigned to your cohorts or cohort depending on which cohort is selected.

On Track: Students who have completed the course.

Performance: Students who have Registered,Started and Completed the course.

Facilitators assigned: The number of facilitators you have assigned that have facilitator access to your institutional account.


Cohort Progress stacked bar chart

X axis lists all of the cohorts you have created and the y-axis is a count of the number of learners in that cohort by status of registration and completion of assignments.

  • Started = Learner has viewed at least one page of content.
  • Not Yet Started = Learner has registered but has not viewed any content.
  • Completed = Learner is registered and has completed all tasks assigned
  • Not Registered = Learner has not completed user registration process
