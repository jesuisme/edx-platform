What is missing from the following goal statement to make it a SMART goal?

Decrease 30-day readmission rates for cardiovascular patients discharged from the 5th floor cardiac care floor by 5%.



It isn’t attainable
It isn’t measurable
It isn’t time-bound

Question 1 of 3

Incorrect.

Because it specifies a percentage-decrease goal as well as the location, this is a measurable SMART goal.
Try again

Correct!

When setting a SMART goal, it’s important to add a time frame such as, “Decrease readmission rates for cardiovascular patients discharged from the 5th floor cardiac care floor by 5% within 6 months.” This will help to give your project a deadline and frame your reporting and data testing intervals.
