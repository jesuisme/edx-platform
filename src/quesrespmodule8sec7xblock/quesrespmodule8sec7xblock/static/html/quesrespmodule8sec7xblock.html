In a study of handwashing promotion strategies, two messages were chosen to see which would be more effective. Based on what you’ve read about nudge, which message do you think was more effective?

Hand hygiene prevents you from catching diseases
Hand hygiene prevents patients from catching diseases

Question 1 of 3

Incorrect.

Much of nudge theory plays off individuals’ perceptions of group morality and how their actions effect and are judged by those groups.
Try again

Correct!

According to the article in Psychological Science, providers tended to believe in their own immunity, and were more likely to change behavior in order to benefit their patients.
